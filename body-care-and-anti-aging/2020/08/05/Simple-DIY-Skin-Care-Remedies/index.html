Simple DIY Skin Care Remedies

Maintaining a healthy skincare regimen can be a bit tricky. People often accidentally abuse their skin without realizing it. There are many branded cleansers, toners, moisturizers, and anti-aging products that contribute to skin abuse. If you find that many over-the-counter products leave your skin dull, dry, flaky, and you never get the long-term results you want. Many products even cause serious breakouts, as most OTC products are only made to protect against sunburn and nothing else.

The other reason lies in the chemicals the products are made of. If you read the ingredients on the bottles carefully, some of these ingredients are used in the making of cleaning products, bleaches, and disinfectants that may not be healthy for your skin at all. This is where accidental abuse comes in and we continue to abuse it because we believe covering our skin with makeup will fix the problem. The goal should not be just beautiful skin, but healthier skin. Below are some of my own DIY skincare techniques and secrets that will help you achieve your goal of healthier skin and a healthy, radiant glow.

It is also your best weapon against bacteria and inflammation that cause blackheads. Witch hazel is a natural astringent. It helps reduce the risk of inflammation, and its powerful anti-inflammatory properties are great at killing bacteria that build up under your pores even before they start.
